Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Welcome to SCRITT. We are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle information in compliance with Google Play Store Developer Policies.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and profile pictures (via Supabase Auth).
  • Listing Data: Item titles, descriptions, prices, and locations.
  • User Content: Images uploaded for marketplace listings and payment receipts for verification.

2. Device Permissions

To provide our services, SCRITT may request access to:

  • Camera & Photo Gallery: To allow you to take or upload photos of items you wish to list for sale.
  • Location: To help you tag your items with specific areas (e.g., Abeokuta districts) for local buyers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate buying and selling between users.
  • To verify payments and prevent fraudulent listings.
  • To communicate service updates or security alerts.

4. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (a backend-as-a-service provider) to store your data securely. Your data is encrypted and stored in professional-grade data centers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your information as long as your account is active. Account Deletion: You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can initiate this within the app settings or by contacting us via email.

6. Safety & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage users to use strong passwords and protect their login credentials.

7. Children's Privacy

SCRITT is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
